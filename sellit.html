What is it all about

A lot of Information Systems students & Computer science students have a lot of system prototypes they have developed running in their local environments in their PCs. What if we just develop a marketplace where they can sell these systems and make money. SellIT is an innovative marketplace for selling systems, websites, code snippets as well as dissertation documents. Its simply a way for IT students to make money on campus. It will have sophisticated and secure APIs for copyrights.
